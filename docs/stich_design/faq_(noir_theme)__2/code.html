FAQ

AI & Workouts

How does the AI personalize my workouts?

expand_more

Our advanced AI analyzes your performance, feedback, and goals to adjust your training plan daily. It considers factors like your energy levels, muscle recovery, and progress to ensure every workout is challenging yet achievable.

Can I track my progress over time?

expand_more

Yes! The app features a comprehensive progress tracking dashboard. You can view your history, personal records, and see visual charts of your strength and endurance gains over weeks, months, and years.

What if I miss a workout?

expand_more

No problem. The AI is designed to be flexible. It will automatically adjust your upcoming schedule and the intensity of your next workout to ensure you stay on track with your goals without overtraining.

Music Integration

Which music services can I connect?

expand_more

We currently support seamless integration with Spotify. Apple Music integration is planned for a future release.

Can the app sync music to my workout pace?

expand_more

While we don't currently match beats per minute (BPM) to your exercise pace, you can easily create and select high-energy playlists to keep you motivated throughout your session. Full playback controls are available on the workout screen.

Account & Subscription

How do I manage my subscription?

expand_more

You can manage your subscription directly through the Google Play Store. Go to your device's account settings, find 'Subscriptions', and you will be able to view, change, or cancel your plan.

What is your cancellation policy?

expand_more

You can cancel your subscription at any time. You will retain access to premium features until the end of your current billing period. We do not offer refunds for partial subscription periods.
