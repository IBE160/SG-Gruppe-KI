AI Training Advisor - Color Palette Preview

This page visually displays the conceptual color palette for your application. Open this HTML file in your web browser to see the colors.

Primary Blue (Core Brand Identity)

#007BFF (Blue-500 - Main)
A clear, medium-vibrant blue.
#0056B3 (Blue-700 - Darker/Active)
A richer, darker blue for states or stronger contrast.
#EBF5FF (Blue-100 - Lighter/Background)
A very light, soft blue for subtle backgrounds or highlights.
#002D62 (Blue-900 - Deepest/Text)
A very deep, almost navy blue for primary text against light backgrounds.

Accent Yellow (Call-to-Action & Highlight)

#FFD700 (Yellow-500 - Main Accent)
A vivid, pure yellow for maximum contrast and energy.
#FFEB3B (Yellow-300 - Lighter Accent)
For subtle hover states or less critical highlights.
#FBC02D (Yellow-700 - Darker Accent)
For active states or when yellow needs to be against another yellow.

Secondary & Neutral Colors

#212529 (Gray-900 - Primary Text)
Near-black for high contrast text.
#6C757D (Gray-600 - Secondary Text/Icons)
Medium gray for less prominent information.
#CED4DA (Gray-300 - Borders/Dividers)
Light gray for subtle UI separation.
#F8F9FA (Gray-100 - Subtle Backgrounds)
Very light gray for cards, sections.
#FFFFFF (White - Clean Backgrounds)
For clean backgrounds, modal overlays, key content areas.

Semantic Colors (Functional)

#28A745 (Success - Green)
For positive actions, completion.
#FFC107 (Warning - Orange-Yellow)
For alerts, caution, soft errors.
#DC3545 (Error - Red)
For critical errors, destructive actions.
#17A2B8 (Info - Cyan/Light Blue)
For informational messages (distinct from primary blue).
