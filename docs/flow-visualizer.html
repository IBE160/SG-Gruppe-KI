AI Training Advisor - UX Flow Visualizer (Phase 1)

This document provides a visual, structured overview of the user flows designed for Phase 1 of the AI Training Advisor application, highlighting key screens, interactions, and implemented UX improvements.

Goal: To provide a clear, secure, and streamlined entry point for both new and returning users.

Screen 1: Welcome / Authentication Gateway

The app's initial entry screen, featuring a compelling visual, main app title, and primary account actions. It sets an energetic and modern tone with its dark theme and vibrant green accent.

  • **Visual Elements:** Background Image (rowing machine), Dark Overlay/Gradient, App Icon/Logo (`neurology` Material Symbol), Main Heading "Your Personal AI Trainer", Sub-heading "Smart, personalized workouts that adapt to you.".
  • **Primary Action Buttons:**
    • `[ Create Account ]` (Prominent, vibrant green background, dark text).
    • `[ Log In ]` (Secondary, dark background, white text).
  • **Social Login:** "Or continue with" separator, `[ Google Icon ]` button, `[ Apple Icon ]` button.
  • **Legal Text:** "By continuing, you agree to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy."
  • **Interactions:**
    • Tap `Create Account`: Navigates to **Screen 2: Email Authentication** (for sign-up).
    • Tap `Log In`: Navigates to **Screen 2: Email Authentication** (for sign-in).
    • Tap `Google Icon`: Initiates Google OAuth flow.
    • Tap `Apple Icon`: Initiates Apple OAuth flow (Phase 2).

Screen 2: Email Authentication (now split into 2A and 2B)

This section details the email sign-up and login screens. The app navigates to the appropriate screen based on user action (from Welcome screen, or "Log in" / "Sign up" links on these screens). Streamlined Email Authentication with a Combined Flow

Screen 2B: Email Login

Inspired by `email_authentication/code.html`

  • **Visual Elements:** Header (`[ Back Button ]`), Main Heading "Welcome back!".   • **Interactions:** Tap `Back Button` returns to Screen 1. Tap `Log In` submits form. Tap `Forgot Password?` initiates flow. Tap `Sign up` link navigates to **Screen 2A: Email Sign-up**.

Flow 2: Goal & Preference Setup (Conversational Onboarding)

Goal: Engage new users in a low-friction process to gather essential data for AI personalization, consistent with the app's new dark aesthetic and green primary accent.

Trigger: Immediately after first authentication for new users.

Onboarding Screen Layout (Common Elements)

  • **Header:** Fixed at top. Visual Progress Indicator using small, rounded circles (e.g., `h-2 w-2 rounded-full bg-primary` and `bg-primary/30`). Back Button (if applicable).
  • **Main Content:** Scrollable chat area.
    • **AI Chat Bubble:** `AI Trainer` (text-zinc-500), `[ AI Avatar ]`, `[ Speech Bubble (bg-zinc-800 dark:text-white) ]`.
    • **User Response Bubble:** `[ Speech Bubble (bg-primary text-background-dark) ]`.
  • **Sticky Footer (Composer / Navigation):** Fixed at bottom. Includes text input field (for "Custom" options) and `[ Arrow Forward Button ]` (large, rounded, `bg-primary text-background-dark`) for "Continue/Next".

Step 1: Welcome & Goal Selection

  • **AI Prompt:** "Welcome! I'm your personal AI trainer. What's your main fitness goal?"
  • **User Input:** Rounded buttons for goals (e.g., `Lose Weight`, `Build Muscle`). Enhance Flexibility in User Input for Goals: `[ Custom... ]` button reveals text input in Sticky Footer.
  • **Example User Response:** "Build Muscle".

Step 2: Time & Frequency

  • **AI Prompt:** "How many days a week do you want to train, and for how long?"
  • **User Input:** Sliders for "Days per week" and "Minutes per session" (aligned with chat style).

Step 3: Equipment

  • **AI Prompt:** "Got it. What equipment do you have access to?"
  • **User Input:** Rounded buttons for equipment options (e.g., `No Equipment`, `Full Gym`). Enhance Flexibility in User Input for Equipment: `[ Specify... ]` button reveals text input in Sticky Footer.

Step 4: Injuries & Limitations

  • **AI Prompt:** "Do you have any injuries or limitations?"
  • **User Input:** Rounded buttons for common areas. Text input field. `(i)` icon explains necessity.

Step 5: Units

  • **AI Prompt:** "Last question. Do you prefer to work with kilograms or pounds?"
  • **User Input:** Toggle switch: `[ kg / lbs ]` (aligned with chat style).

Step 6: Confirmation & Redirection

  • **AI Prompt:** "Perfect! I have everything I need to create your first workout plan."
  • **Interactions:** Automatic redirection to the **Dashboard** after a short pause or on tap of the `[ Arrow Forward Button ]`.

Flow 3: Connect Spotify (Optional Music Integration)

Goal: Allow users to seamlessly integrate Spotify, with clear communication.

Trigger: Tap "Connect Spotify" during onboarding or in Settings.

Screen 1: Spotify Connection Explainer

This interstitial screen appears *before* redirecting to Spotify OAuth, proactively informing the user about the value and required permissions. It features a compelling header image and a clear list of benefits and permissions, consistent with the app's dark theme and vibrant green accent.

  • **Visual Elements:** Top App Bar (Left: `[ Close Button ]` (Material Symbols Outlined: `close`), Center: Heading "Connect Spotify" (bold, white)). Header Image ("An abstract graphic representing sound waves and energy"). Headline "Power Your Workouts with Music", Body Text.
  • **Benefit List (3 items):** "AI-Powered Playlists", "Performance Insights", "Seamless Experience", each with relevant Material Symbols icon (e.g., `psychology`, `analytics`, `music_note`) in primary green, within a rounded `bg-primary/20` circle.
  • **Permissions Section (Boxed):** Heading "To do this, we'll need permission to:", two permission items (`check_circle` icon in primary green, Title, Description).
  • **Sticky Footer (CTA):** Primary Button `[ Connect with Spotify ]` (vibrant green, `text-background-dark`, with Spotify SVG icon). Disclaimer ("We will never post anything without your permission. Read our Privacy Policy.").
  • **Interactions:** Tap `[ Close Button ]`: Returns to previous screen. Tap `[ Connect with Spotify ]`: Initiates external Spotify OAuth flow.

Screen 2: Spotify OAuth (External Process)

User redirected to Spotify's consent page.

  • **Interactions:** User grants/denies permission. Redirects back to app.

Screen 3: Spotify Connection Status & Confirmation

Appears upon return from Spotify OAuth.

  • **Visual Elements:** Success: Header "Spotify Connected!", message, `[ Got It ]`, `[ Explore Music Settings ]`. Failure: Header "Connection Unsuccessful", message, `[ Try Again ]`, `[ Maybe Later ]`.

Screen 4: Post-Connection Features (Settings -> Music & Playback)

Settings section updates to reflect connection.

  • **Visual Elements:** "Spotify Connection Status", Toggles for "Use Session Mix", "BPM Matching". Section for "Music Preferences for AI Mixes" (from Flow 11).

Flow 6: Generate AI Daily Plan

Goal: Allow users to provide real-time context, receive an adaptive AI-generated plan, and maintain control.

Trigger: Tap `[ Generate Today's Plan ]` from Dashboard or auto-generated.

Screen 1: Context Window

Modal/overlay for user to input current state.

  • **Visual Elements:** Header "How Are You Feeling Today?", AI Prompt, Mood/Energy/Sleep Input (emoticons). Context Window Defaults & Smart Suggestions (pre-filled/contextual prompts). Optional Free-Text Input. Action Buttons: `[ Generate Plan ]`, `[ Skip / Use Last Plan ]`.
  • **Interactions:** Tap `Generate Plan` moves to **Screen 2**. Tap `Skip` uses cached plan.

Screen 2: AI Plan Generation (Loading State)

Brief loading screen.

  • **Visual Elements:** Animated Spinner, "AI is crunching the numbers..." message.
  • **Interactions:** Auto-transitions to **Screen 3**. Displays fallback on timeout/failure.

Screen 3: Review Proposed Plan

Displays AI-generated plan for review.

  • **Visual Elements:** Header "Your Daily Plan - Review & Adjust", AI Prompt (explains adaptation). Workout Plan Display (list of exercises, sets, reps, RPE). Visual Representation of Plan Changes (highlights for adjusted elements, tooltips explaining changes). Action Buttons: `[ Confirm Plan ]`, `[ Edit Plan ]` "Edit Plan" Option Before Confirmation.
  • **Interactions:** Tap `Confirm Plan` moves to **Screen 4**. Tap `Edit Plan` opens modal/interface for granular edits.

Screen 4: Plan Confirmed/Saved

Brief confirmation screen.

  • **Visual Elements:** "Plan Confirmed!", "Redirecting to your Dashboard...".
  • **Interactions:** Auto-redirects to the **Dashboard**.

Flow 7: Start Workout (Using AI Plan or Synced Plan)

Goal: Provide a smooth, clear, and prepared transition into the active workout session, starting with a distinct warm-up phase.

Trigger: User taps `[ Start Workout ]` from Dashboard or plan card.

Screen 1: Workout Loading State

Brief interstitial to signal preparation. Visual Confirmation of Plan Loading

  • **Visual Elements:** App Logo/AI Icon, "Loading your personalized plan...", subtle spinner.
  • **Interactions:** Auto-transitions to **Screen 2**.

Screen 2: Pre-Workout Preparation / Warm-up Introduction

Transition and preparation hub. Explicit "Warm-up" Phase Start

  • **Visual Elements:** Header "Ready for your workout?", Summary Card, AI Prompt. If warm-up in plan: "Today's Warm-up:" section, list of exercises, `[ Start Warm-up ]`. If no warm-up: `[ Start Workout ]`. Secondary options.
  • **Interactions:** Tap `Start Warm-up` moves to **Screen 3**. Tap `Start Workout` (no warm-up) moves to **Screen 4**.

Screen 3: Active Workout Player (Warm-up Phase)

Active workout environment for warm-up.

  • **Visual Elements:** Prominent "WARM-UP" label, current warm-up exercise, instructions, countdown timer, `[ Next Exercise ]` / `[ Finish Warm-up ]`.
  • **Interactions:** User completes exercises, taps `Next`. Tap `Finish Warm-up` moves to **Screen 4**.

Screen 4: Active Workout Player (Main Workout Ready)

Core workout logging interface, ready for first main exercise.

  • **Visual Elements:** Prominent "MAIN WORKOUT" label, current exercise, target sets/reps/RPE. Integrates elements detailed in Flow 9 UX.

Flow 9: Perform & Log Workout

Goal: Provide an intuitive, efficient, and adaptable interface for users to execute and accurately log their workout sets.

Trigger: User is inside Workout Player and begins progressing through exercises.

Screen: Active Workout Player (Main Workout View)

  • **Top Bar:** `[ Exit/Pause ]`, Workout Title, `[ Overall Workout Progress Bar ]` Visual Cues for Progression.
  • **Main Content (Current Exercise Card):** Exercise Name, Target, Instructions, `[ Current Set Progress ]` Visual Cues for Progression.
  • **Logging Controls:**
    • Optimized Input for Reps/Weight/RPE: Large `[ + ]` / `[ - ]` buttons, RPE slider/buttons, smart pre-fill.
  • **Action Button:** `[ Log Set ]`.
  • **Rest Timer Section (after Logging):** Countdown timer.
    • Clear Next Exercise/Set Preview: Prominent "Next: [Exercise Name] - Set X of Y". `[ Skip Rest ]`, `[ Extend Rest (30s) ]`.
  • **Workout Controls (via icon/menu):**
    • Integrated Mid-Workout Modification UI: `[ Edit Current Plan ]` option for quick granular edits.
    • `[ Enable/Disable Audio Cues ]` (for improvement below).
    • `[ End Workout ]`.
  • Audio Cues/Guidance: Configurable audio cues for rest timer end, new exercise start, motivational prompts.

Flow 10: Finish Workout & AI Feedback

Goal: Provide a rewarding and insightful conclusion, summarizing achievements and delivering actionable AI guidance.

Trigger: User taps `[ Finish Workout ]` from Active Workout Player.

Screen 1: Post-Workout Summary & Feedback Input

  • **Visual Elements:** Header "Workout Complete!", Basic Stats (Duration, Volume, Sets/Reps). Feedback Prompt "How did that feel?". Input for Perceived Fatigue/Overall RPE (visual scale), Optional "Quick Notes." Action Button: `[ Get AI Feedback ]`.

Screen 2: AI Feedback & Achievements Display

  • **Visual Elements:** Header "Your Session Insights". Highlight Key Insights/Achievements ("Celebrate Wins" section with PRs, milestones, streaks, animations). Provide Actionable AI Feedback (AI Coach Summary/Recommendation). Detailed Metrics. Next Steps / Action Buttons: `[ Go to Dashboard ]`, `[ Share Workout ]`, `[ Edit Workout Log ]`.

Flow 11: Generate Session Mix (Optional AI-Based Playlist)

Goal: Provide personalized, AI-generated music that adapts to workout phases, with review options and real-time feedback.

Trigger: Pre-Workout, Post-Workout, or Settings -> Music & Playback.

Settings Screen (Music & Playback Section): For Preference Input

  • **Visual Elements:** "Spotify Connection Status", Toggles for "Use Session Mix", "BPM Matching". "Seed" Playlist/Genre Preference Input (Preferred Genres, Favorite Workout Artists, Overall Energy Level, Exclude Artists/Genres).

Screen 1: Generate Session Mix Options (Modal/Overlay)

  • **Visual Elements:** Header "Generate Session Mix", AI Prompt, Playlist Mode Selection (`[ Warm-up only ]`, `[ Full Session ]`, `[ Recovery / Cooldown ]`), `[ Create Mix ]`, `[ Cancel ]`.

Screen 2: Session Mix Preview & Customization

Displays AI's proposed tracklist for review and adjustment. Pre-Generation Preview & Customization

  • **Visual Elements:** Header "Review Your Session Mix", AI Prompt. Tracklist Display (with `[X]` Remove, `[Refresh]` Replace icons per track). Action Buttons: `[ Start Mix / Play Workout ]`, `[ Generate New Mix ]`, `[ Cancel ]`.

Screen 3: Active Music Playback (within Workout Player - Flow 9)

  • **Visual Elements:** Music Control Widget. Visual Feedback on BPM Matching (BPM Indicator showing current song's BPM vs. target phase BPM, color-coded, textual feedback). `[ Device Selector ]`.

Flow 13: Edit Synced or Completed Workout

Goal: Provide a flexible and robust way to correct or refine workout logs post-completion.

Trigger: User taps `[ Edit ]` on a completed workout from history.

Screen 1: Workout Details & Edit Mode Entry

  • **Visual Elements:** Header "Workout Details", Workout Summary (read-only), `[ Edit Workout ]` button.

Screen 2: Granular Editing Interface

  • **Visual Elements:** Header "Edit Workout", Save/Cancel Bar. Editable Workout Details (Workout-level, Exercise-level, Set-level). "Undo" Option for Edits (Floating `[ Undo ]` button). Visual Diff for Versioned Logs - Secondary Access (`[ Version History ]` button/link).
  • **Interactions:** User makes changes. `Undo` reverts. `Save Changes` processes, handles conflicts.

Screen 3: Version History & Visual Diff (Accessed via `[ Version History ]`)

  • **Visual Elements:** Header "Workout Version History". Version List. Comparison View (Side-by-side diff or inline highlighting). Action Buttons: `[ Restore this Version ]`, `[ Compare with Current ]`.

Flow 14: View Dashboard & Weekly Review

Goal: Provide a personalized, motivating, and interactive overview of training progress and adherence.

Trigger: User opens Dashboard or navigates to "Weekly Summary".

Screen 1: Main Dashboard View

  • **Visual Elements:** Top Bar (`[ Menu/Profile ]`, Workout Title, `[ Generate Today's Plan ]`). "Celebrate Wins" Section (prominent, dynamic section for PRs, milestones, streaks). Customizable Dashboard Widgets (Widget Grid/Layout, `[ Customize Dashboard ]` icon). Example Widgets: Benchmarking & Goal Progress Visualization (`[ Goal Progress Widget ]`), `[ Weekly Volume Trend ]`, `[ Workout Streak ]`, `[ RPE Trend ]`, `[ Recent Workouts ]`, `[ Today's Context Summary ]`. Bottom Navigation.
  • **Interactions:** Scrolls, taps widgets (links to Screen 2), taps `Customize Dashboard`.

Screen 2: Weekly Review / Detailed Insights

  • **Visual Elements:** Header "Weekly Review", Timeframe Selector. Interactive Data Exploration (Interactive Charts - Volume, Intensity, Consistency. Tap data points for details, drill-down to workouts). AI Insights & Recommendations.
  • **Interactions:** User adjusts timeframe, interacts with charts. Back button returns to Screen 1.

Screen 3: Customize Dashboard (Settings)

  • **Visual Elements:** Header "Customize Dashboard", "Available Widgets List", "Current Layout Preview" (draggable representation). `[ Remove ]` / `[ Drag Handle ]` for widgets. `[ Save Layout ]`, `[ Reset to Default ]`.

Flow 15: Export Data (GDPR Compliance)

Goal: Provide a clear, transparent, and secure process for users to export their personal data.

Trigger: User navigates to `Settings -> Privacy & Account -> Export Data`.

Screen 1: Export Data Information & Request

  • **Visual Elements:** Header "Export Your Data", AI Prompt. Clear Scope of Exported Data (Phase 1 Focused) ("What's Included (Phase 1):" bullet list, exclusion statement for wearables). Process Explanation. Action Button: `[ Request Data Export ]`.

Screen 2: Export Initiated Confirmation

  • **Visual Elements:** Header "Export Request Received!", Confirmation Icon, AI Prompt. Estimated Time for Export Generation ("Your export may take up to [e.g., 5-10 minutes]"). Delivery Method. Action Button: `[ Go to Settings ]` or `[ Go to Dashboard ]`.

Flow 16: Delete Account (Full GDPR Removal)

Goal: Provide a secure, transparent, and irreversible process for users to permanently delete their account.

Trigger: User navigates to `Settings -> Privacy & Account -> Delete Account`.

Screen 1: Delete Account Warning & Export Reminder

  • **Visual Elements:** Header "Permanently Delete Your Account?" (Red text), Warning Icon, AI Prompt ("This action cannot be undone."). Impact Statement (Phase 1 Focused). GDPR Reminder. Last Chance to Export (link to Flow 15). Action Buttons: `[ Continue to Delete ]`, `[ Cancel / Go Back ]`.

Screen 2: Final Confirmation with User Input

Enhanced Confirmation Steps with User Input

  • **Visual Elements:** Header "Confirm Account Deletion" (Red text), Final Warning. AI Prompt ("Type 'DELETE'"). Confirmation Input Field. Re-enter Password Field (Optional). Action Button: `[ Permanently Delete My Account ]` (disabled until correct input).

Screen 3: Deletion Initiated Confirmation & Logout

Clear Deletion Timeline & Communication

  • **Visual Elements:** Header "Account Deletion Initiated", Confirmation Icon, AI Prompt. Deletion Timeline ("removed within [e.g., 30 days]. Email confirmation..."). Action Button: `[ Okay, Log Me Out ]`.
  • **Interactions:** User is immediately logged out, redirects to **Screen 4**.

Screen 4: Completion Screen (Logged Out State)

  • **Visual Elements:** App Logo, "Your account deletion process is underway.", `[ Return to Login ]`.

Flow 17: Toggle Offline Mode & Resume Sync

Goal: Ensure robust app functionality and data preservation even without an internet connection.

Trigger: User enables "Offline Mode" in Settings or loses/restores connection.

Settings Screen (Performance & Data Section): For Offline Mode Configuration

  • **Visual Elements:** Toggle `[ Offline Mode ]`. Automatic Sync with Notification (Toggle `[ Auto-Sync Offline Data ]`, with description).

General App UI: Offline Status Indicator & Sync Now Button

  • **Visual Elements:** Clear Offline Status Indicator (Persistent banner stating "Offline Mode" or "No Internet Connection"). Easily Accessible "Sync Now" Button (on banner, or Dashboard/History, or Settings).

Sync Prompt (on reconnection, if Auto-Sync is OFF)

  • **Visual Elements:** Modal/Toast: "Internet Restored!", "You have offline changes...", `[ Sync Now ]`, `[ Review Changes First ]`, `[ Not Now ]`.

Confirmation/Notification (Post-Sync)

  • **Visual Elements:** Toast message: "Offline changes synced successfully!" or "Sync failed."

Flow 18: Change Settings (General, Health, Playback & Privacy)

Goal: Provide a comprehensive, intuitive control panel for personalizing the app experience.

Trigger: User taps `[ Settings Icon/Menu Option ]`.

Screen 1: Main Settings Menu

  • **Visual Elements:** Header "Settings". Search Functionality for Settings (`[ Search Settings... ]` input). List of Setting Categories.

Sub-Screens (Examples): Appearance, Performance & Data, AI & Personalization, Privacy & Account

  • **General Elements:** Header, Back Button. Settings Toggles/Dropdowns/Inputs.
  • Contextual Help & "Learn More": `[ (i) ]` icons next to complex settings with tooltips.
  • Preview for Appearance Settings (in Appearance Sub-Screen): Live preview for theme/color changes.
  • Confirmation for Destructive Actions: Confirmation modals for actions like `[ Clear Local Cache ]` or `[ Reset All AI Learning ]`.
  • Transparent AI Preference Management (in AI & Personalization): "Learned Preferences/Constraints" lists with `[ Edit ]` / `[ Delete ]` options.
  • Prominent Logout Option (in Privacy & Account): `[ Logout ]` button with confirmation.
